# Design System: Deep Focus & Creative Clarity

## Concept
A personal "Sanctuary" for deep work and intellectual growth. The design balances clinical precision with organic warmth, inspired by dark-mode IDEs and high-end botanical journals.

## Theme Tokens
- **Primary Color:** Emerald 400/500 (`#34d399`) - Represents growth and cognitive vitality.
- **Surface:** Deep Slate/Teal (`#0d1518`) - Minimizes eye strain and promotes focus.
- **Typography:**
  - **Headings:** Serif (e.g., 'Inter' with high tracking or a custom Serif font) - Evokes a sense of classic scholarship.
  - **Body:** Inter (Sans-serif) - Ensures readability for long-form note-taking.
- **Shape:** Round 8px - Modern, approachable, and soft.

## UI Principles
1. **Glassmorphism:** Use of subtle blurs (`backdrop-blur`) and thin borders (`border-white/10`) to create depth without visual noise.
2. **Focus-First:** Floating toolbars and contextual sidebars that recede when not in use.
3. **Data Visualization:** Using the Knowledge Graph and Momentum metrics to gamify the learning experience.

## Component Specifications for AI Context
- **Sidebar:** 3-column layout capability for desktop; Collapsible for mobile.
- **Editor:** Markdown-supported, distraction-free interface with floating formatting bars.
- **Graph:** Node-link diagram style with glowing emerald nodes.
